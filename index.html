Welcome to my website!

This is Rachel's world of data, where I let my solid data skills, curiosity for the universe, 23 years of wisdom (* and narcissism) run wild and free. Willingly or not, here you will see a geek's research and projects on advertisement industry data, text mining, high dimensional statistical models, and edgy(?) data visualizations. Proudly, I opened the blog section, where I share my trouble-shooting experience using R, Python and other languages, which hopefully can give something back the data science community. I also have a Chinese blog, where I keep my deepest darkest little secrets -- but if you don't know Chinese, too bad -- cause it's quite entertaining. If you haven't clicked on the little red cross on the top right corner, (or left, for Mac snobs), then congratulations for discovering the first ad-free website since Justin Bieber was born.
